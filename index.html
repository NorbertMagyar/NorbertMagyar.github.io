Welcome to my personal GitHub page! Here you will find information about my past-present-future projects, professional and side-projects alike.

About me: I am an astrophysicist, plasma physicist, and software engineer. Currently, I build scientific and production software at the intersection of high-performance computing, cloud-native systems, scientific AI, and space weather applications for the European Space Agency (ESA).

Selected Work

  • ESA Space Weather Payload Data Centre (backend/cloud-native engineering)

    As a core developer, I take part in designing and implementing the center's cloud-native backend services for scientific data ingestion and processing, building distributed microservices (Java/Spring Boot, messaging, databases), deploying containerized infrastructure with Kubernetes and GitOps, and implementing observability stacks (ELK, Grafana).

  • HPC plasma/MHD simulations (AMRVAC, AthenaK, FLASH)
    Please check my other, research-oriented website for a showcase of my research, including simulation movies.
  • International Space Science Institute (ISSI) team (Bern, CH)
  • AI-driven space weather forecasting pipelines (based on the open-source Surya foundation model for heliophysics)
